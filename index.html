Our Last Summer

Mountain Hike!

First we all took a trip to the northern parts of Norway and hiked. it was summer, but the snow could still be seen atop the mountains.

Approaching the last peak of our trip we stood up and took a great picture we will all remember forever

Driving with Andreas

On our trip through Norway we met a guy named Andreas. This person was a proffesional driver, or so he said. He tought us how to speed on the highway without getting caught and also how to evade the police if they were on our tail.

Since then we got a mail from Andreas a few weeks back where he said that he was put in prison! We were all suprised and are still wondering how such a thing could've happened.

Game night

When we reached back home for a break we decided to have a game night.

We stayed up from 8pm to 8am and all crashed on the couch the day after.

Italy

In the middle of the summer we went to Italy together, we flew the day after gamenight, in the afternoon.

Arriving in Italy was great fun, we ate pizza, saw Italian cinema and bathed. The pizza did indeed have that thin crust everyone talks about, and the tomato sauce was astonishing.

Pigs in Italy

Pigs in Italy are suprisingly well taught!

We went to a circus were they had pigs who could talk, sort of, they made noises whenever they were given food. Maybe that is just what pigs do.

Last day

The last day of summer we spent on a beach in Norway, enjoying the evening sun.

We all had a great summer, and were looking forward to university in just a few days.
